# Privacy Policy

## 1. 引言  
## 1. Introduction  
本隐私政策解释了当您使用 Track of Habits 应用时，我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。Track of Habits 是一款离线打卡应用，我们不会主动收集您的个人数据。为维持应用运营，本应用接入了第三方广告服务，该服务可能收集相关设备与广告交互信息。  
This Privacy Policy explains how Track of Habits collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app. Track of Habits is an offline check-in application, and we do not actively collect your personal data. To support app operations, this app integrates third-party advertising services, which may collect relevant device and ad interaction information.

## 2. 我们收集的信息  
## 2. Information We Collect  
Track of Habits 不会收集您的任何个人数据。我们不会追踪您的位置、收集您的电子邮件地址，也不会收集其他任何个人数据。该应用完全离线运行，无需联网。  
Track of Habits does not collect any personal information from you. We do not track your location, collect your email address, or gather any personal data. The app is designed to function entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

## 3. 本地数据存储  
## 3. Local Data Storage  
Track of Habits 使用 Core Data 在您的设备上本地存储您的打卡数据，包括：  
Track of Habits uses Core Data to store your check-in information locally on your device, including:  

- **打卡项目名称**：您创建的打卡项目信息  
- **Check-in Project Names**: Information about the check-in items you create  

- **打卡记录**：您标记的打卡完成状态和时间  
- **Check-in Records**: Status and time of your check-in completions  

- **设置偏好**：应用内的个性化设置  
- **Settings Preferences**: Personalized settings within the app  

所有这些数据都存储在您的设备本地，我们不会访问、存储或传输这些信息到任何外部服务器。  
All this data is stored locally on your device, and we do not access, store, or transmit this information to any external servers.

## 4. iCloud 数据同步  
## 4. iCloud Data Sync  
应用使用 iCloud 在您的 Apple 设备间同步打卡数据：  
The app uses iCloud to sync your check-in data across your Apple devices:  

- **数据同步**：您的打卡数据通过 Apple iCloud 在设备间同步  
- **Data Sync**: Your check-in data is synced across devices via Apple iCloud  

- **必需功能**：iCloud 同步是应用正常运行的必要功能  
- **Required Feature**: iCloud sync is a necessary function for the app to operate properly  

iCloud 数据同步遵循 Apple 的隐私政策和服务条款，我们不对 iCloud 服务的隐私做法负责。  
iCloud data sync is subject to Apple's privacy policy and terms of service, and we are not responsible for the privacy practices of the iCloud service.

## 5. 数据存储  
## 5. Data Storage  
所有数据都存储在您的设备本地并通过 iCloud 服务同步。我们不会将您的打卡数据上传、共享或出售给任何第三方。数据始终由您控制。  
All data is stored locally on your device and synced through the iCloud service. We do not upload, share, or sell your check-in data to third parties. The data remains under your control at all times.

## 6. 第三方广告服务  
## 6. Third-Party Advertising Services  
为维持应用免费运营，本应用接入了第三方广告服务。广告服务可能收集以下信息：  
To support the free operation of this app, we have integrated third-party advertising services. The advertising service may collect the following information:

- **设备信息**：设备品牌、型号、系统版本、屏幕分辨率、设备语言、时区、设备标识符等  
- **Device Information**: Device brand, model, system version, screen resolution, device language, timezone, device identifiers, etc.  

- **网络信息**：运营商信息、Wi-Fi 状态、网络信号强度、IP 地址  
- **Network Information**: Carrier information, Wi-Fi status, network signal strength, IP address  

- **应用信息**：应用版本号、应用前后台状态  
- **Application Information**: App version number, app foreground/background status  

- **广告交互数据**：广告展示、点击、转化等交互数据  
- **Ad Interaction Data**: Ad impressions, clicks, conversions, and other interaction data  

- **性能数据**：崩溃数据、性能数据（用于保障服务稳定性）  
- **Performance Data**: Crash data, performance data (for service stability and reliability)  

上述信息用于广告投放、广告监测及服务稳定性保障，不能单独识别特定自然人身份。如您希望关闭个性化广告推荐，可在应用内选择关闭。  
The above information is used for ad delivery, ad monitoring, and service stability. It cannot alone identify a specific individual. If you wish to disable personalized ad recommendations, you may select to turn it off within the app.

## 7. 安全性  
## 7. Security  
我们采取合理的措施来保护存储在您设备上的数据免受未经授权的访问。然而，请注意，没有任何数据存储系统是完全安全的。您有责任保护您的设备以及存储在设备上的数据。  
We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored on your device from unauthorized access. However, please note that no data storage system is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your device and the data stored on it.
